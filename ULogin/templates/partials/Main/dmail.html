What is Dmail?

Disposible Email

Tired of spams or disclosing your email addresses???
Majority of sites will require you to give them your email ids for registering with them. If your are one to hate such thing them Dmail is for you.

Dmail or the disposible email is one which can be given to sites or people for temporary usage like registration. Dmail have no password associated with it. Any one can check the mails to its any ids after giving them to sites for registration.

For eg: you can give anonymous@usignin.com (or anyname@usignin.com) to any sites and get the activation link they send by checking mails to that id in our Dmail inbox.

This will eliminate the need to
1. reveal your accounts.
2. need of login to them.
3. chance of spamming them.
