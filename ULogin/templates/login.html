VirtualKeyboard

Virtual Keyboard allows you to enter your passwords with out the fear of being tracked. This will ensure you protection from keyloggers.They are programs running the background to track the keys pressed and notify the person who installed them about the info you typed. Thus you have a high chance of loosing your passwords when accounts are accessed from insecure system like that in internat cafes, which may unavoidable while travelling.Our virtual keyboard eliminates such possibilities. Once you enter the site using VirtualKeyboard you can access all your accounts in just one-click

If you dont want to create account or store your account details with us,you can try out our OpenUVL which provides one-click login with VirtualKeyboard.
OpenID

OpenID is a free and easy way to use a single digital identity across the Internet. When UVLogin allows you to manage all your password protected accounts, OpenID will make even registration on many sites unnecessary. With OpenID ie. "username.uvlogin.com" you can login to a large number of sites without registering in any of them.
Check out the sites supporting OpenID

  • The OpenID Directory
